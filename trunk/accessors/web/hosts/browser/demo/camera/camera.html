Camera for browser, beta version.
  1. You may see a pop-up error: ""No parameter named camera for accessor with id Camera". Click "OK".
  2. Scroll down, click "react to inputs".
  3. When the prompt appears, select a webcam and click "Share Selected Device". The video feed will appear at the page top left.
  4. Under "Inputs", enter true for "trigger" and click "react to inputs".
  5. A still image will be captured and appear at page top right.
