Welcome to the Speech Recognition demo!
This demo ONLY works in CHROME.
  1. Scroll down to "Inputs". Click "react to inputs".
  2. Say something. The text should appear under "Outputs", "text".
  3. Speech recognition ends after a phrase has been detected.
  4. To operate continuously, change the "continuous" parameter to "true" and click "react to inputs".
  5. Say a few phrases. Each phrase text should appear under "Outputs", "text".
  6. To stop recognition, under "Inputs", enter "false" for "start", enter "true" for "stop" and click "react to inputs".
