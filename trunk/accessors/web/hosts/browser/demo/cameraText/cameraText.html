Read text from your webcam!
  1. Scroll down to "Inputs". Click "react to inputs".
  2. A dialog box will pop up. Select a webcam and click "Share Selected Device".
  3. The video feed and a still image will appear.
  4. Hold up some text to the webcam. Make sure the video feed contains only text.
  5. Click "react to inputs". The detected text will appear under "Outputs", "text" output.
